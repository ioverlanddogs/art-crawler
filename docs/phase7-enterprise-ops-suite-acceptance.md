# Phase 7 Acceptance Criteria

Phase 7 is complete when all are true:

## Tenancy + teams
- operator can safely switch tenant/workspace context
- ownership and assignment are visible
- queues and dashboards clearly reflect current scope
- cross-team handoff is understandable

## SLA / SLO
- at-risk and breached work is obvious
- overdue queues are visible
- trend and MTTR context is readable
- no critical SLA state relies on color alone

## Team workflows
- assignment and reassign flows are clear
- ownership is explicit
- shift handoff context is visible
- overloaded teams are identifiable

## Reporting
- leadership KPIs are understandable at a glance
- trend summaries are visible
- exception and reversal trends are explicit
- reporting degrades gracefully with partial data

## UX
- tenant and SLA states are textual
- keyboard and focus behavior remain accessible
- partial telemetry does not break scoped views
